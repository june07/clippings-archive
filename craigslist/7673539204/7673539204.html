"A Craigslist flag marks Craigslist R&R post as inappropriate, and if

"Craigslist automatically monitors the IP addresses of where posts originate,

YOU ARE PARTIALLY CORRECT.

-Any CL posting can be 'flagged' by the general public. (including but not limited to Rants & Raves)

-Many people now use a "VPN" which disguises their "true" IP address. (To everyone. Including CL !)

from which to 'flag' other peoples' postings right off of the CL website.
